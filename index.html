App Installation

Bonus Bridge is a Progressive Web App that you can install on your device for easier access.

1
Open this page in your device's browser

Make sure you're viewing this on the device where you want to install the app.

2
For iPhone/iPad:

Tap the share button (square with arrow) at the bottom of Safari, then scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".

3
For Android:

Tap the menu (three dots) in Chrome, then tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".

Launch Bonus Bridge App
Note: The trial version allows 5 deals. Contact Mike Smith for extension codes.

About Bonus Bridge

A scoring application for contract bridge with both standard and bonus scoring options.

Use the app to track scores during your bridge games without the need for paper scoresheets.
