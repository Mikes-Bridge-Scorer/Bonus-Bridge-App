About Bonus Bridge

Are you tired of the same old bridge games where luck often outweighs skill? Looking for a fresh approach that rewards strategic thinking and skillful play?

Bonus Bridge is an innovative scoring system designed to transform your home games, cruise ship tournaments, or friendly gatherings into a more engaging and competitive experience.

What Makes Bonus Bridge Different?

Created by long-time bridge enthusiast Mike Smith, Bonus Bridge preserves everything you love about traditional bridge while introducing a sophisticated scoring system that rewards skill over luck.

Unlike standard bridge where being dealt good cards gives an automatic advantage, Bonus Bridge balances the playing field by rewarding strategic bidding and skillful play.

App Installation

Bonus Bridge is a Progressive Web App that you can install on your device for easier access.

1
Open this page in your device's browser

Make sure you're viewing this on the device where you want to install the app.

2
For iPhone/iPad:

Tap the share button (square with arrow) at the bottom of Safari, then scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".

3
For Android:

Tap the menu (three dots) in Chrome, then tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".

Note: The trial version allows 50 deals with a reminder appearing at 60% usage (30 deals). Contact Mike Smith for extension codes.
